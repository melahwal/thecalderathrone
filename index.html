THE CALDERA THRONE

A Premium Original Political War Fantasy Series

War Through Systems. Power Through Silence.

A five-book political war fantasy about legitimacy, memory, food, records, prophecy, and the cost of rule — where wars begin in ledgers before they reach the sword.

The Caldera Throne is an adult epic political war fantasy series by Mustafa EL Ahwal, built around power, legitimacy, prophecy, ledgers, logistics, royal succession, and institutional collapse. Across The Still and the Burning, The Empire of Ledgers, and What the Mountain Kept, the series explores how wars are shaped not only by swords and armies, but by records, supply lines, silence, and systems.
