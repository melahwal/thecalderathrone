ABOUT THE AUTHOR

Mustafa EL Ahwal

By day, Mustafa EL Ahwal works in the intricate world of finance — a career that taught him how the quiet wheels of power and governance actually turn. But at his core, he is a builder.

In his quiet hours, Mustafa crafts detailed miniature houses, finding peace in the patience required to weave small elements into a living world. That same care breathes life into The Caldera Throne. He builds his story layer by layer, from the vast sweep of kingdoms to the weight of a single private choice.

Much of his work was born in the deep silence of the desert. A lover of solo camping, Mustafa often writes under the open night sky, where the vastness and solitude of the land help shape the atmospheric soul of his novels.
