CHARACTERS

A gallery of rulers, survivors, tacticians, and quiet instruments of change.

Adrian

Royal-blooded blacksmith and strategist drawn toward power he never wanted.

Mai

Princess of Marya and disciplined commander carrying a kingdom’s grief toward return.

Elyanna

Living bridge between the mainland and the island after thirty years of silence.

Marcus

Intelligent but ruinous ruler who mistakes obedience for legitimacy.

Helena

Queen mother, palace memory, and dangerous keeper of buried truths.

King Aurelius

Fading legitimate king whose quiet theft cracks the island’s future.

Tomas

Butcher, father, and moral weight of ordinary resistance.

Greger

Dwarf baker, loyal friend, sharp witness, and the story’s honest mirror.

Livia

Pattern reader who turns records, routes, and anomalies into intelligence.

Rorik

Guard captain whose broken oath becomes a moral fracture inside the palace.

Rhia

Captain of Greyhook, practical, controlled, and clear-eyed under pressure.

Cassandra

Helena’s sister, sacrificed by trust and weaponized by Marcus after death.

Azadeh

Daryan princess trapped inside a system she understands better than those using it.

King Shen

Maryan king whose grief becomes policy and whose memory rebuilds a fleet.

King Bahram

Daryan king who reads margins, records, and old debts better than speeches.

Jian

Survivor of the drowned fleet and navigator of waters, truth, and silence.

Rustom

Young Daryan prince and operational bridge between mainland and island.

Ashbourne

Elegant political operator who turns access, records, and service into leverage.

Draven

Varkan warlord who rules through fear, debt, and controlled fury.

Varric

Varkan architect and intelligence mind guarding the mountain’s buried secrets.

Kaito

Maryan general whose doctrine turns aggression into duty.

Akira

Noble-born social weapon who damages Mai through timing, tone, and procedure.

Arvand

Administrative coup-maker who controls reality through records, stamps, and delay.

Babak

Arvand’s corridor instrument, dangerous in small rooms and quiet thresholds.

Prince Kael

Draven’s son, desperate to prove himself, whose infiltration wounds the palace.

Varyn Ironfist

Master of War, blunt soldier and survivor of shifting loyalty.

Kellus

Master of Coin whose final warning leaves Livia the key.

Giv

Bahram’s silent guard, loyal as stone and dangerous before danger becomes visible.

The Twins – Turgut & Turan

Ruthless twin enforcers feared for their violence, loyalty, and brute force.

Arash

Azadeh’s quiet witness who preserves the record before it can be buried.

Ilva

Varkan child who reads the mountain’s signs before others can name them.

Elara

Tomas’s wife, holding the stall, the child, and herself together.

Mira

Tomas’s daughter, the human reason behind his strength.

Narseh

Debt-burdened clerk corrupted through one deniable corridor conversation.

Saburo

Mai’s convoy commander, precise, disciplined, and impossible to distract.

Rellin

Palace guard captain whose cold obedience ends under Marcus’s gaze.

Sorush

Relay architect who builds resilience into the intelligence network.

Drosan

Varric’s senior captain, executor of decisions and recorder of cost.

Namik

Archive clerk who preserves the full verse where history forgets to look.

Oru

Kaishō harbor master who turns grief into work and keeps building.
