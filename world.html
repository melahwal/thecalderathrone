THE WORLD

Sea, silence, memory, and the systems that decide who survives.

The Caldera Throne spans a known world of volcanic island power, Maryan return, Daryan calculation, and the southern imperial pressure of Sarkath, divided by sea, memory, and thirty years of silence.

Aurelians / Mor Vallas

Island royalists shaped by legitimacy, hunger, and ancestral fracture.

Varkans / The Crags

Power-brokers guarding trade routes, military pressure, and buried truths.

Maryans / Kaishō

Commanders and state-builders turning prophecy into operational advantage.

Daryans / Mehrakan

Mainland rulers balancing honor, council politics, and old alliances.

Sarkath

A southern imperial power built on tribute, classification, arsenals, and administrative conquest — a force that does not simply invade, but restructures what it touches until control looks like routine.
